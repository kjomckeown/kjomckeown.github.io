About

My name is Kathy Jo (KJo) McKeown. I am a photographer enticed by both the simplicity and complexity of my surroundings. While the subject matter of my photography is quite diverse, I mostly enjoy shooting full-frame close-up images found in everyday life. I embrace the challenge to capture and convey to others what my eyes have been privileged to see.
